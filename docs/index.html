cd contacts2notion
uv sync
uv run contacts2notion auth

About

contacts2notion is a personal project by Michael Demar. It's designed to keep your contacts synchronized between Google Contacts and Notion, with smart merging and Notion-only metadata fields.

All data stays between your Google account and your Notion workspace - no third-party servers involved.
